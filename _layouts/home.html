I’m Siddharth, a digital product designer based out of Bangalore, IN. I work almost exclusively with startups and founders — helping them design / build beautiful products from the ground up.

Having no formal design education, everything I’ve learnt has been the culmination of nearly a decade’s worth of working on design and dev projects, large and small, from across the globe. Though I focus primarily on UX/UI design, I’m equally passionate and profficient with frontend and backend dev, branding, strategy, communication and product. This gives me an unique edge when working with complex projects involving multiple disciplines.

Good design is hard. Great design is invisible. The journey so far has taught me that great work is the result of passion, curiosity, consistency, conviction and skill. The way I strive to work couldn’t be better encapsulated than the phrase imparted by the late Steve Jobs.

Stay hungry. Stay foolish.
